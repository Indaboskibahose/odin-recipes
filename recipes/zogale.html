Kwadon Zogale

Zogale is a popular salad recipe in northern Nigeria

Zogale is a salad made from moringa leaves and kuli-kuli

Ingredients:

  • Moringa
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • onion
  • 2 tbsp of oil
  • kuli-kuli as required

Steps:

  1. Remove moringa leave from the stems and wash thouroughly, steam in a pot for 20-25mins until well cooked
  2. Then cut all the veggies finely, and transfer the steamed moringa into a large bowl, add the veggies, oil, cube seasoning and lots of kuli kuli, mix well and serve
